Information About Mountaineering

American Alpine Club (AAC):

The AAC is a renowned organization that supports climbers and promotes responsible mountaineering practices. Their website offers a wealth of information on climbing ethics, safety, expeditions, and community resources.

Mountain Project:

Mountain Project is a popular online resource for climbers, featuring an extensive database of climbing routes, trail information, gear reviews, and user-generated content related to climbing destinations around the world.

American Mountain Guides Association (AMGA):

The AMGA is a professional organization for mountain guides and climbing instructors. Their website provides resources on guide training, certification, industry standards, and educational opportunities for aspiring and experienced guides.

SummitPost:

SummitPost is a community-driven website that offers detailed route information, trip reports, photos, and discussions for various mountains and climbing destinations worldwide. It's a valuable resource for climbers seeking firsthand experiences and insights.

REI Co-op Journal - Climbing Section:

REI's Co-op Journal includes a climbing section that covers a range of climbing-related topics, from how-to guides and gear recommendations to inspiring narratives and information about climbing destinations.
